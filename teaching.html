Funds
  • August 2023 - September 2024
    Innovedum Fund
    ETH Zürich - Zurich, Switzerland

    The Innovedum project, initiated by Dr. Dr. Jacopo Tani and guided by Prof. Emilio Frazzoli and Dejan Milojevic, aims to enhance the educational experience for the 300+ students who take this course each year. While control systems are inherently theoretical, their real-world applications are vast and impactful. To bridge theory and practice, we introduced interactive Jupyter notebooks, enabling students to tackle real-world implementation challenges. Through these notebooks, students explored and applied various control techniques like LQR, LQG, H2, H∞, and MPC to case studies involving Duckiebots, airplanes, Morse code and robot grippers.
